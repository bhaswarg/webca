please read these carefully

Tournament Entry Fees

-Tournament Entry Fees Fees are advertised on a per tournament basis. Some tournaments have an entry fee of credits, while some tournaments are free!

Creating a Team

-Each team will have a Team Captain who is responsible for creating the team and entering the tournament scores. Once a team is created, teammates can then join the team by entering the Team Pin during registration.

The Team Captain can choose to register/pay for theirself, or for theirself and teammates up front. Upon checking out, Team Captains will receive a Team Pin that should be kept private and shared only with teammates. The full registration process is detailed on each tournament's registration page. Responsibilities of the Team Captain are as follows:
-Team name
-Division selection
-Time window selection
-Team streaming link
-K/D Threshold requirements
-Team Pin distribution

General Rules

-Crossplay must be enabled at all times.
-Gametime tourneys take cheating very seriously, and we have implemented various tools to make our tournaments fair and competitive. If it is discovered that you have used any 3rd party cheating software, or used any exploits that provide a competitive advantage in any way, your entire team will be disqualified from the current competition and may be banned from future tournamens
-If there is a draw between teams, the team with the highest kill game out of the 3 submitted wins.
